Hi, I'm Dr. Maya Reynolds

I'm a licensed clinical psychologist based in Santa Monica, California, specializing in helping adults who feel stuck in anxiety, overwhelmed by stress, or impacted by past trauma.

Many of my clients are high-achieving professionals who appear "fine" on the outside but quietly struggle with constant worry, overthinking, emotional exhaustion, or the lingering effects of earlier experiences.

My approach is warm, collaborative, and grounded in evidence-based methods including CBT, EMDR, and mindfulness practices. I create a calm, supportive space where you can slow down, reconnect with yourself, and develop lasting tools for well-being.
